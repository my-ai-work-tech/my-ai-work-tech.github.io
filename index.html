Episode 1: The American Ouverture

00:00

It's remarkable how certain moments retain their vividness, even after a decade has passed. Ten years ago, the opportunity arose to finally cross the threshold into a country I had long held in keen curiosity: the United States of America. For someone with an explorer’s heart, this wasn't just a trip; it was the realization of a quietly held aspiration to experience a truly new cultural and physical landscape.

What added a truly profound layer to this journey was the destination itself: the chance to reconnect with my son, then embarking on his own professional life there. I arrived not just as a casual tourist, but as a parent ready to share in a new chapter, eager to see the world through his contemporary lens.

I remember the initial anticipation—a blend of calm composure masking an intense, youthful excitement. The flight itself was the passage into a dream I'd nurtured for years. But what did I discover upon touching down on that vast continent?

Would you be interested in hearing about the very first, perhaps unexpected, sensory impression I had upon arriving in the USA?
